Chunjiang Zhu, Ph.D.

Chunjiang Zhu received his PhD at the Department of Computer Science from City University of Hong Kong in 2015. He is now a postdocter fellow at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering in the University of Connecticut starting from April 2018. Before that, he was a postdocter at the City University of Hong Kong and Hong Kong Baptist University for a few months, and also worked in the Hong Kong based Wisers AI Lab as a Researcher and a Senior Researcher.
